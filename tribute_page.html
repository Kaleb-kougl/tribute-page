A timeline of Stefani Joanne Angelina's life

  • 1986 - Born in New York City, U.S.

  • 1997 - Accepted to Julliard at the age of 11!
  • 1999 - Wrote her first ballad

  • 2003 - 1/20 Accepted early to NYU, later dropping out to pursue music full-time

  • 2007 - Began working with Interscope Records as a songwriter
  • 2008 - The Fame hit the radio and was nominated for a Grammy Award
  • 2009 - Just Dance hit no. 1 on Pop Charts
  • 2009 - The Fame Monster album was released
  • 2011 - Born This Way was released
  • 2013 - ArtPop was released
  • 2014 - Lady Gaga collaborated with Tony Bennet releasing a Jazz album, Cheek To Cheek
  • 2014 - She won a grammy for Best Traditional Pop Vocal Albumn
  • 2015 - Starred in Amercan Horror Story - Hotel
